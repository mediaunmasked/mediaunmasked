Topics Discussed

  • Aperture (depth of field)
  • Shutter speed (motion blur)
  • ISO (noise/grain)
  • Color profiles and image settings (RAW, compressed)
  • Frame rate

Tips

When shooting video, make the shutter speed 2x the frame rate. To avoid (handheld) blur, make your shutter speed around the same number as the focal length on the lens.
